Don't freak out if you chose one product, you finish the delivery and got back in delivery post and see the customers name is still there!

🔍 The Issue:
You delivered DIFFERENT PRODUCTS to Joe and Sarah:

Documents (product 3) → Sarah (customer 4)

Electronics (product 4) → Joe (customer 3)

So when you select Pizza (product 2) , Joe and Sarah are STILL available because they never received Pizza!

so YEAH it depends on the products now!

The NO-REPEAT system blocks per product, not per customer overall.

📊 How it works:
Customer	Pizza (ID 2)	          Documents (ID 3)	       Electronics (ID 4)
Alex (1)	❌ Never delivered	    ❌ Never delivered	    ❌ Never delivered
Maria (2)	✅ Delivered at 14:58	❌ Never delivered	    ❌ Never delivered
Joe (3)	    ❌ Never delivered	    ❌ Never delivered	    ✅ Delivered at 16:00
Sarah (4)	❌ Never delivered	    ✅ Delivered at 15:58	❌ Never delivered

🎯 This means:
Select Pizza → Available: Alex, Joe, Sarah (Maria blocked for Pizza only)

Select Documents → Available: Alex, Maria, Joe (Sarah blocked for Documents only)

Select Electronics → Available: Alex, Maria, Sarah (Joe blocked for Electronics only)

✅ This is CORRECT behavior!
A customer can:

Receive Pizza today ✅

Receive Documents tomorrow ✅ (different product)

Receive Electronics next week ✅ (different product)

They just can't receive the SAME product twice within 1 hour.

"محصول یکسانی" (same product)

Not "any product" - so your system is 100% correct!
